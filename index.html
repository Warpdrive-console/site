What is Warpdrive

Built Different.
On Purpose.

🌐
Open Source Runtime

Web games via Chromium with WebGPU. Native ARM binaries later down the line for full-performance titles, and the freedom to choose your game engine. From unity, or GMS2, to Scratch, GBStudio, and GDevelop, with dozens of tools in-between.

🎮
Real Culture

The console Developers grew up with Newgrounds, Itch.io, and Gamejolt. PS3, Xbox360, and Wii modding culture. Open firmware. Moddable frontend, and a homebrew launcher out of the box. Warpdrive wouldn't exist without console-modding, and the freedom to experience gaming culture from a young age. It's only natural that the carry-over is massive.

💿
Physical Media

Full-size DVD in purple collector cases. Warpdrive has a disc drive for phsyical games, as well. It's nothing flashy, and it isn't the fastest, but it exists for those who want it. For the phsyical media enthusiasts, collectors, or those without Wi-Fi. And hey, you can install the discs to storage, too.
